Common questions

Will my data sync to a new phone?
Yes — CalorieCalc syncs through your private iCloud account. Sign in with the same Apple ID on a new device and your history will appear.

Do I need an Apple Watch?
No. CalorieCalc works on iPhone alone. If you have an Apple Watch, workouts and active energy from it will flow in via Apple Health.

Can I export my data?
Yes — Settings → Export gives you a full CSV of your foods, weights, workouts, and goals.

Is the AI accurate?
AI-generated nutrition is an estimate, not a measurement. Always review and adjust before relying on the values.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Open the Settings app on iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → CalorieCalc → Cancel.
